## Task 3: Ackley函数优化

### 10维Ackley函数
$$f(x) = -20\exp\left(-0.2\sqrt{\frac{1}{10}\sum_{i=1}^{10}x_i^2}\right) - \exp\left(\frac{1}{10}\sum_{i=1}^{10}\cos(2\pi x_i)\right) + 20 + e$$

### 优化结果
- 全局最优: f(x*)=0 at x*=[0,...,0]
