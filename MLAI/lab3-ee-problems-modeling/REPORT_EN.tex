\section{Task 3: Ackley Function Optimization}

10-dimensional Ackley function:
\begin{equation}
f(x) = -20\exp\left(-0.2\sqrt{\frac{1}{n}\sum x_i^2}\right) - \exp\left(\frac{1}{n}\sum\cos(2\pi x_i)\right) + 20 + e
\end{equation}

Global optimum: $f(x^*)=0$ at $x^*=[0,...,0]$

GA achieved: $|f(x^*) - 0| < 0.01$
